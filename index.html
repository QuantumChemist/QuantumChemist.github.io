About me
Dr. Christina Ertural Curriculum Vitae (CV)

Hi, I'm Christina Ertural, an active scientist in the theoretical and solid-state chemistry community, currently PostDoc at BAM in Berlin in the junior group of Dr. Janine George and former LOBSTER developer. Scroll down for more information about me and my research.

My research interests involve different areas of theoretical, quantum and computational chemistry such as the development and improvement of well-established and innovative methods. Another important aspect in my research is the application of different quantum chemical approaches to solid-state and materials chemistry concerning compounds like Zintl phases, especially thermoelectrical materials, cathode and anode materials for battery technology or phase-change materials.
